Your Book Request Journey

Curious about what happens after you request a book? Here’s a simple breakdown of our process to get the books you need into your hands.

1

Browse & Request

You fill out the form with details about the book you're looking for. The more specific you are, the better!

2

We Review & Source

Our team receives your request and checks its availability from our network of suppliers and donors.

3

Prepare for Delivery

Once sourced, we process the book and get it ready for pickup or delivery to your location.

4

Receive & Enjoy

You get notified that your book is ready. Now it's time to dive in and enjoy your new read!
